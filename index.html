About Our Church

Welcome to our small but vibrant church family. We are a part of the Indian Christian Pentecostal Church (ICPF) organization, dedicated to spreading the message of Jesus Christ and serving our local community. Our mission is to provide a place of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth for everyone. We believe in the power of faith and the strength found in coming together as a community.
